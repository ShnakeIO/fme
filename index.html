📖 How to Play Fightin Mortal Enemies

🎯 Objective:

Collect skeleton keys and explore all 1000 magical rooms! Fight epic bosses and upgrade your powers!

🎮 Controls:

Desktop: WASD to move, Mouse to aim and shoot

iPad/Mobile: Arrow keys to move, select power then tap screen to shoot

⚡ Powers:

❄️ Ice Beam | 🔥 Fire Beam | ⚡ Combined Beam | 🌟 Lightning Storm (unlocked from bosses)

⚔️ Boss Fights:

Every 5 keys triggers an epic boss battle! Fight 10 different boss types that cycle through. Win to upgrade one of your powers permanently!

🔥 Power Upgrades:

After defeating a boss, choose to upgrade Ice (+20 damage), Fire (+25 damage), or Combined (+40 damage) with faster cooldowns!

💎 Power-ups:

❤️ Health | ⚡ Speed | 💥 Damage | 🛡️ Shield | 🚀 Mega Speed | ⭐ Invincibility
